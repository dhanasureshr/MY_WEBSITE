Sonicsyncsail Music Player App

Sonicsyncsail is a feature-rich music player app that supports local playback, radio streaming, and synchronized playback across devices. Key features include:

  • Dynamic playback queue management
  • Music effects like equalizer and bass boost
  • Radio station recording and playback
  • Sync playback using Direct WiFi
  • Customizable sleep and wake-up timers
  • Seamless mode switching between local, radio, and sync modes
